<p></p>
<ol data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Students must be neatly dressed in their school uniform when they come
to school.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students are to be in school attire when they come to school for supplementary
/ remedial / enrichment lessons / CCA.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students can only be in their P.E. attire on the days they have P.E. lessons,
except Primary 1 and Primary 2 students. School-based T-shirts can be worn
in place of the P.E. T-shirt only when approved by the school management,
for example Camp T-shirts, Level T-shirts, CCA attire, etc.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students must wear plain black shoes with school socks. Sports shoes are
not allowed except on designated days when there are sports events in the
school.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students must wear name tags on their uniforms and P.E. attire. Name tags
should be sewn just above the pocket for the current uniform or at the
'L'-shaped placeholder marking for the new uniform. For P.E. attire, the
name tag should be sewn above the school badge on all T-shirts.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students are not allowed to wear jewellery such as rings, bangles, bracelets
and necklaces to school. Girls may wear simple and small ear studs. Multiple
earrings are not allowed. Boys are not allowed to wear any ear studs /
earrings.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students are to ensure that their nails are short and clean. They should
not apply nail polish on their nails.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Students are to refer to the images below for the correct way to wear
the school uniform and P.E. attire.</p>
<p></p>
</li>
</ol>
