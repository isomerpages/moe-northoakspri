The school sent a team of three Primary 2 Chinese students to participate in the annual Chinese Moo-O Awards 2018 held in May 2018 at Princess Elizabeth Primary School. The students were taught how to use the Moo-O software to create an interactive story on the day of the competition. The team was awarded the Merit award for their articulation, expressive and creative reading. Congratulations to Josash (2E), Krystine Lee and Shao Xuan (2Frangipani)!
