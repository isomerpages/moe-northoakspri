The school sent a team of three Primary 3 students to participate in the annual Moo-O Awards 2018 held in March 2018 at CHIJ OLGC. The students were taught how to use the Moo-O software to create an interactive story on the day of the competition. The team was awarded the Gold award for their articulate, expressive and creative reading. Congratulations to Joshua, Nainika and Ajay!
